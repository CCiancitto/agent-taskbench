# Safety and Scope

AgentTaskBench is intended for local demos and benchmark examples that do not require secrets or private data.

## Safe Default

- keep examples synthetic
- keep validation local
- keep the repo dependency-light
- keep the task scope explicit

## Do Not Use For

- secrets
- private repositories without understanding the tooling risks
- regulated or sensitive data
- destructive operations

## Scope Discipline

Every example should make it obvious what the agent may change and what it must leave alone.
